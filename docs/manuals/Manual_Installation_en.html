Installation Guide

Add to Home Screen

Transform the Web App into a native application on your mobile device.

For Android Devices (Chrome)

1
Open the site in Chrome Navigate to our App's address using the Google Chrome browser on your smartphone.
2
Open the Options Menu Tap the three dots (⋮) icon in the top right corner of the Chrome bar.
3
Install the App Select "Install app" or "Add to Home screen".
4
Confirm Tap "Add" in the window that appears. The App icon will instantly appear on your home screen alongside your other applications.

For iOS / iPhone Devices (Safari)

1
Open the site in Safari It is essential to use Safari on iPhone to correctly install the App.
2
Tap the Share icon Press the Share button (the square icon with an upward arrow) located in the bottom bar.
3
Add to Home Scroll down the list of options and tap "Add to Home Screen".
4
Complete the operation Tap "Add" in the top right corner. The FOREXcross icon will appear on your iPhone's home screen.
Why install it? Once added to the home screen, the App will open without the browser bars, providing a faster, smoother, and more professional full-screen experience, just like an application downloaded from official Stores.
